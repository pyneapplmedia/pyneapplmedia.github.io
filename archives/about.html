The Pyneappl Mission

Our mission is pineapples.

We are Pyneappl Media. We are an unofficial media company that is very free roaming in what we do. We have interests in games, music, apps, videos, space exploration, and pineapples of course. We don't really stick to one thing over the other. Since we're not a real company, at this time, we don't accept donations of any sorts, nor do we want any money.

We're not in it for money. We're more focused on sharing our creations on a free and open source platform, and we're being humble. Right now anything monetary related is coming out of free subscriptions or our own pockets. Maybe our mission will change in the future, but right now, we just want to share our creations, and provide a free entertainment service, at no cost to us.

Where do we see ourselves in a few years?

Right now, our (unofficial) company is still in the making! We don't know where we'll be years from now, right now we're doing this for fun, and to share what we make, and for our love for pineapples.

If we were to say where we'd be in a year from now, we wouldn't be able to answer honestly, so for now, we'll opt out of answering that question until we've gotten a steady hold in whatever world we'll be in. We hope for the best of our small (still unofficial) company, and will see it grow in whatever form it molds into.
