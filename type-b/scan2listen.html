Project Scan2Listen

Scan your entire CD library without worrying about ripping CDs.

It's a work in progress.

You scan your CD's barcode

You scan the barcode on the back of your CD case. It then looks up the corresponding song and puts it in a playlist.

You import the playlist

So long as you have a valid premium subscription with Spotify, you will be able to import all of the songs on your Scan 2 Listen playlist to Spotify.
