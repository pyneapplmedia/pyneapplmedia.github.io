PyneapplGaming FAQ

Can my/our community get a game server?

No, not at the moment, as we really are only working with one community at the moment.

How much does it cost to run your game server?

Nothing at the moment. We try and spend little to no money on servers. We were working with a host, but our free trial ran out at the moment.

Who hosts/controls the servers? 

PyneapplGaming servers are hosted by the owner(s) of Pyneappl Media, or by Trusted Hosts.

Trusted Hosts are individuals that host our game servers for us.

We ideally hope that our Trusted Hosts share in our mission, as seen on our About us page. 

TRUSTED HOST AGREEMENT

(We tried our best to make this as short and straight to the point as possible)

By becoming a Trusted Host, you agree not to receive payment for your services. You agree to share joint administrative rights to the server with the owners of Pyneappl Media

Trusted Hosts may maintain confidentiality of the means of hosting their server (payment method, owner account details). Pyneappl Media retains no rights to such information, and will not ask for it.

Trusted Hosts must promise, to the best of their ability, that they have the proper rights to their server, and if it is a VPS or Hosting Website, the payment method was used to aquire such server, was used through legitimate, rightful means.

Pyneappl Media certifies that it's server will be available to the community through a sub-domain designated by Pyneappl Media. (i.e. bananacommunity.pyneappl.media)

Trusted Hosts agree not to share the root IP address or domain of their designated game server with the community.

Trusted Hosts hold full ownership of their game server, or as designated by the Trusted Host's hosting company. The Trusted Hosts must designate the owner(s) of Pyneappl Media as administrators, and must give them control of any resources that will be public through the "pyneappl.media" domain or it's subdomains, as well as the control panel necessary to maintain the server.

Trusted Hosts agree to maintain the server from an ownership standpoint where Pyneappl Media has no access to. (i.e. No SFTP access, no SSH access, etc.)

Pyneappl Media agrees to administrate the server for the community, and allow certain designated community staff to perform at least moderator-like responsibilities.

Pyneappl Media retains the right to stop forwarding services from the Trusted Host to the community at any time.

Trusted Hosts retain the right to stop providing services to Pyneappl Media at any time.
